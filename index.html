Luck Test

Time :- 00:00


Result Board

Step 1: First, enter the player name and the amount of money you want to bet in the input box.

Step 2: A box will open in your name. In that box, you have to select a number. This number will be matched with the lucky number.

Step 3: After selecting the number, click the Update Number button. When you click this button, you will be added to the game round.

Step 4: Click the Check Result button. The results of all players will be shown in the result section.
